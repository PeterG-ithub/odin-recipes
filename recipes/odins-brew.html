Odin's Brew

Description

A special blend of rich, dark roast coffee infused with a hint of magical spices and served with a dash of the mythical elixir of immortality, the Mead of Poetry.

Ingredients

  • 1 cup strong, dark roast coffee (brewed)
  • 1/2 teaspoon ground cinnamon
  • 1/4 teaspoon ground cardamom
  • Pinch of ground nutmeg
  • Pinch of ground cloves
  • Pinch of ground ginger
  • Optional: 1-2 teaspoons honey or sweetener of your choice
  • Optional: Milk or cream (dairy or plant-based) for added richness

Instructions

  1. Brew a cup of strong, dark roast coffee using your preferred method. You can use an espresso machine, French press, pour-over, or any other method you prefer.
  2. While the coffee is brewing, combine the ground cinnamon, ground cardamom, ground nutmeg, ground cloves, and ground ginger in a small bowl. Mix the spices together to create a spice blend.
  3. Once the coffee is ready, pour it into your favorite mug.
  4. Sprinkle the spice blend over the hot coffee, adjusting the amount to your preference. Stir the coffee gently to incorporate the spices.
  5. If desired, add honey or your preferred sweetener to enhance the flavor. Start with 1-2 teaspoons and adjust according to your taste.
  6. For added richness, you can add a splash of milk or cream to the coffee. Stir well to combine.
  7. Odin's Brew is now ready to be savored! Embrace the aromatic blend of spices and the robust flavor of the dark roast coffee, as you experience the strength and wisdom of Odin.
